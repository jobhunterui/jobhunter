Welcome to Job Hunter Assistant

Your personal assistant for finding and applying to jobs

How Job Hunter Works

Job Hunter Assistant helps you manage your entire job search process from start to finish. The extension has three main tabs:

1. Find Jobs: Search for job opportunities across multiple platforms with tailored search features.

2. Apply: Save and manage job postings, then generate customized applications.

3. Profile: Store your CV and create tailored applications for each job.

Getting Started

To get the most out of Job Hunter:

1 First, set up your Profile by pasting your CV in the Profile tab.

2 Use the Find Jobs tab to search for opportunities.

3 When you find a job posting you like, right-click on the page and select "Save Job to Job Hunter".

4 Access your saved jobs in the Apply tab and generate customized applications.

Basic Job Search

Enter your desired role, location, and experience level to search for jobs. Click any of the search buttons to find jobs on different platforms:

• Find on LinkedIn: Search LinkedIn's job listings directly.

• Find on LinkedIn Feed: Search LinkedIn's feed for job announcements using hiring phrases.

• Find on Job Boards: Search across major job application systems (Lever, Greenhouse, etc.).

Advanced Search Features

• Find in Docs and Pages: Discover job opportunities in Google Docs, Notion, and other document platforms.

• Find with AI: Use AI to search for highly relevant job opportunities with detailed information.

• Hiring Phrases: Customize the hiring phrases used for searches by clicking "Show hiring phrases". Add your own custom phrases for better results.

Saving Jobs

When you find a job posting you're interested in:

1 Right-click anywhere on the job posting page.

2 Select "Save Job to Job Hunter" from the context menu.

3 The job will be automatically saved to your Apply tab.

You'll receive a notification when a job is successfully saved.

Managing Applications

In the Apply tab, you can manage all your saved jobs:

• View Details: Click the "View Details" button to see full job information.

• Generate Application: Select a job and click "Generate Application" to create a tailored CV and cover letter (requires your CV to be saved in Profile tab).

• Remove: Select a job and click "Remove" to delete it from your saved jobs.

Important: You must have your CV saved in the Profile tab before you can generate applications.

Setting Up Your Profile

1 Paste your complete CV text in the "Your CV/Resume" textarea.

2 Click "Save Profile" to store your CV.

This step is essential before you can generate applications. Your CV information will be used to create tailored applications for each job.

Generating Tailored Applications

After saving your CV and finding a job:

1 Select a job in the Apply tab and click "Generate Application".

2 The extension will format a prompt and open Claude.ai in a new tab.

3 Paste the prompt into Claude.ai and get your tailored application.

4 Copy the JSON response from Claude back to the extension in the "Tailored CV JSON" field.

5 Click "Preview CV" to see your beautifully formatted CV and cover letter.
